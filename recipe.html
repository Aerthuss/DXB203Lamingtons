Notes before cooking:

  • - You need to cook the cake the day before to make sure that it has enough time to cool down and dry out a bit.
  • - If your eggs are very large, you might need to increase the amounts of the other ingredients to compensate.
  • - Fun tip: Add some food colouring (a few drops) to your sponge for a colourful surprise.

Sponge Cake Recipe

Ingredients

Please check that you have all required ingredients before cooking!





Method

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 180C. Grease a 20cm x 30cm shallow cake pan.

Step 2: Cream together the sugar and butter in a mixing bowl. They should achieve a creamy texture with quite a thick consistency. The longer, harder and faster the butter and sugar are creamed the lighter the cake will be when cooked.

Step 3: Add the vanilla essence and the eggs 1 at a time, mixing well after you add each egg.

Step 4: Add the flour and mix in well. Beat with a wooden spoon trying to incorporate as much air as possible.

Step 5: Spoon the mixture into the prepared pan and smooth out the top so it is level.

Step 6: Bake for approximately 30 minutes or until a skewer stuck in the centre comes out clean. Turn off the oven but leave the cake inside to cool down slowly for 10 minutes.

Step 7: Remove the cake from the oven and turn out on to a wire rack to cool. Cover with a tea-towel and leave overnight.


Icing Recipe

Ingredients

Please check that you have all required ingredients before cooking!




Method

Step 1: Sift the icing sugar and cocoa into a bowl. Add butter and boiling water. Stir until smooth.

Step 2: Cut cake into approximately 5cm squares. Place coconut in a dish. Dip the sides of the cake in the icing mixture. Shake off any excess icing then roll in the coconut.

Step 3: Place on a wire rack to set (approximately 2 hours) before serving.
